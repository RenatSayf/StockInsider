Part One. Introduction.

Most brokers have a very undervalued tool by traders - CFDs on shares of American companies, but almost no one uses it. And very in vain! These instruments are the most predictable precisely because of the open actions of smart money in these instruments. Well, let's take it all in order.

A little bit about the benefits. The most important CFD on stocks has no expiration date, i.e. we will be able to hold this contract without time limits, often it is quite important point. But you have to pay for the good. The broker charges a commission and a spread on the contract for this, but these are insignificant amounts, they pay off without any problems. For example, if you trade CFDs on futures contracts, then they have an expiration date (expiration date), so you need to close your position and open on a new project - and this is another double spread + double commission.

Next, the CFD does not provide for physical ownership of the asset, which means that it disappears the need to supply the asset, and with it various additional costs, commissions and risks. Then there are small deposit requirements, unlike American brokers the stock market. As well as available margin trading, the collateral, on average, is only 10%.

And of course - convenience. We can trade these instruments from our favorite MetaTrader with all its advantages. There is no need to study another platform and get used to it. I think enough about CFDs, let's spend more time getting to know smart money.

So, who is smart money? It is customary to call people (or organizations) that they know what others do not know, and confidently make money on it. Somehow very mysterious, mysterious and illegal, what do you think? But in fact it's not really so.

I do not dispute that there are many illustrative examples of illegal use of insider information. information, but there is also a 100% legitimate part. This is the part we will learn use it for personal gain. So let's remove this curtain mystery and mystery.

So who is the insider. This is a person or organization that has access to information that is not available to the general public. There is nothing in the very concept of "insider" criminal, there can be a crime only in cases of illegal use of this information about this is just below.

Here we have just come to the question of why we will trade CFDs on American stocks. The fact is that it is the United States that has the strongest control legislation actions of insiders. Each insider, after making a transaction, must report to the regulatory body, namely the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). And the most the main thing is that this information is available for use.

For reference. The Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) is a government agency The United States, which is the main body that regulates and supervises the the American securities market. The Commission was established quite a long time ago, back in 1934. The goal The commission's creation was to restore investor confidence in the stock market.

Let's not get ahead of ourselves until we get back to the insiders. Under US law insiders are called directors and employees of the company, as well as shareholders who own 10% or more of the company's shares. Well, here I think it's clear who owns what information "firsthand". What kind of information can it be that can affect the price shares (or in our case, the CFD price). For example, it may be the signing of some an important contract that affects the company's prospects. Also, the insider is the first to know about a possible takeover of the company, he knows long before the publication of the reporting on his companies and so on, this list can be continued indefinitely. In one word, only an insider knows the real state of the company, whether it is healthy and promising or, on the contrary, there are big problems in the company at this stage of development.

Since then, when insiders are required to report on transactions, others investors often watch these deals. In addition, there is a separate concept investing, in which the behavior of insiders is given a leading role. Just this one concepts, we will follow. Of course, there may be a risk that an insider acquires stocks specifically to increase investor confidence or sells stocks contrary to financial the state of the company (for example, to diversify assets or to buy a house), but all we will already consider this in the following parts.

Part Two. Reporting.

In the first part, we learned that every legitimate insider after the transaction must report to the Securities and Exchange Commission (let me remind you, he oversees and regulation of the securities market). They do this according to a special form that it is called Form 4 SEC (or simply Form 4). This form is immediately after filling out published on the commission's website - www.sec.gov in the "FILINGS" section. Well, this is so for information, we will not read and process hundreds of different documents in search of the right of course, there are special sites for this, which do it for us for free, but more on that later. Let's first analyze Form 4, look at it below in the figure.

We have a small table in front of us, in which it is very conveniently done - the form itself is black, and the filled data is blue. So the form is well readable.

The structure of all completed forms is the same. So, let's go straight to decoding the form, and let's start with the title table. By points (according to the table):

  1. The name of the person who filled out the form, place of work and address. This is insider data, who submits the form
  2. Name the name of the company for which the insider transaction was made. As well as her ticker (this is a short name, the same ticker will be in your list of CFD instruments for stocks)
  3. The date of the earliest transaction. There may be several purchases or sales in the form, here the date of the very first transaction is indicated
  4. Correction to the form. It sometimes happens that the form is filled out with errors. Therefore, in order to to publish the correction of the error, you need to fill out form 4 again, and just in this the paragraph indicates the date of the first, erroneous form
  5. The relation of the person who fills out the application to the issuer. This is what we talked about in the first part, which insider. It can be the director of the company, some her other employee, the owner of 10% of the company's shares, or another person - in the stock below is detailed
  6. Type of application - one person, or a group of persons fills out the form

As we have seen, a fairly simple table. Now consider Table 1 - "Non-derivative securities" (Table I - Non-Derivative Securities Acquired, Disposed of, or Beneficially Owned), which is information important for us:

  1. The name of the type of security - here it will almost always be "common stock" - ordinary stocks.
  2. Transaction date 2A is an extended field 2, in principle, it does not matter for us.
  3. Transaction code. For us, only "P" - purchase, from the English word purchase, and "S" - sale of shares (from sale) are important. Other options are possible here, but they do not have for we have no weight.
  4. The number of shares, then the code (it is not important to us) and the price at which the operation took place.
  5. The number of shares owned by the person after the transaction. Here we can see, for example, if an insider sold the entire remaining package, the figure will be zero.
  6. The form of ownership of shares is direct or indirect. A straight line is when a person who filled out a form, personally owns shares, indirect - this is, for example, a person owns shares through some kind of fund, etc. Well, in general, it's also not particularly important, because an insider wants to make a profit regardless of whether he owns shares directly or indirectly.
  7. The nature of indirect ownership of shares, filled in exactly how the person owns the shares, who filled out the form. Also, not an essential field.

Table 2 - Derivative Securities (Table II - Derivative Securities Acquired, Disposed of, or Beneficially Owned), here we are talking about options, warrants, convertible securities. There are similar fields here, there is no need to stop at this table it doesn't make much sense, it's basically not used for trading.

And even below there is an item "Explanation of Responses" (notes) - here there is an explanation to footnotes of the completed form. This point sometimes has weight, but I haven't considered it for a long time it, and this does not prevent you from earning, in principle, you can also miss it.

Below, as in all documents, the insider's signature and the date of filling in. That's the whole form. Nothing complicated, but a lot of useful.

From this particular form 4, we see that the insider is Williams Gates (more familiar to us as Bill Gates), who is one of the directors of Microsoft (also, I think everyone we are also familiar with this company) sold 4,761,637 shares of his company on February 14, 2014 at an average price of $37.6062 per share, and on February 18, 2014, he sold another 6 488,363 shares at an average price of 37.5289.

Следующий момент. На отдельной акции не будет каждую неделю сигналов, но благодаря тому, что у дилинговых центров довольно большие списки CFD на акции, то торговать всегда будет что. Кстати, данная стратегия очень хорошо подходит для тех, кто не имеет возможности постоянно сидеть у монитора. Хватит один раз в день посмотреть сделки инсайдеров, и если есть что-то интересное, то сделать изменения на своем счету и все. Данная стратеги очень близка к инвестированию.

Еще важный момент, данная стратегия отлично работает для покупок CFD на акции, но она не будет работать для продаж. Почему? Все просто. То, что инсайдер покупает, мы все понимаем - с целью получения прибыли, но вот с продажами все иначе. Для продаж могут быть разные причины, например, он (инсайдер) хочет заплатить по кредиту, или ему срочно надо купить новое жилье и т. п., поэтому продажа инсайдером акций не всегда будет значить, что это пик роста, во многих случаях это не так. Поэтому, я крайне не рекомендую входить в короткую позицию тогда, когда продает инсайдер, а использовать его продажи только для закрытия своих покупок.

И последний момент. При открытии счета для торговли CFD на акции выбирайте swap-free счета. Дело в том, что в большинстве случаев сделку надо держать не одну неделю, и нет смысла терять на свопах деньги (иногда немалые), особенно если есть возможность не платить за перенос позиции через ночь.
